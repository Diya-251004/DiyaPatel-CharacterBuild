Tokyo - The Narrator

Tokyo, originally known as Silene Oliveira, is the main character who narrates Money Heist. She is a well-experienced thief who is part of the heist crew. Initially, a fugitive on the run was introduced, Tokyo, then gets involved in the heist by the Professor.

Her impulsiveness and steadfast determination, which are her trademarks, blend with the weakness she shows and the strength she possesses. Throughout the series, while Tokyo experiences a close bond with the rest of the robbers, she sometimes experiences moral dilemmas about their criminal activities.

x

Professor - The Mastermind

He is called The Professor, but his real name is Sergio Marquina; he is the mastermind of the Money Heist bank heists. Having a genius-level intellect and precise planning skills, The Professor handles the whole operation from backstage by navigating his team to the success with an impeccably planned strategy.

Besides his calm bearing, the meticulous attention to detail, and moral ambiguity make him the character as he tries to overcome the ethical dilemma of his actions. The Professor develops a profound relationship, especially with Tokyo, which adds to his character by showing that his cold side is preceded by his weakness.

Rio - The Young Idealist

Rio, who is in real life Aníbal Cortés, is a young cyber geek and one of the most emotionally charged figures in the series. He came into the picture as Tokyo's love interest, and his youthful idealism is often at variance with the older, more experienced members of the crew.

Rio is among the people who are good at technology and hacking, thus contributing to the heist, but at other times his thoughts that are not realistic come up with reckless decisions. Throughout the development of the series, he struggles with the consequences of their actions, and his feelings toward Tokyo are evident, hence showing how his character dabbles between innocence and maturity.

Nairobi - The Strong-Willed Leader

Nairobi, whose actual name is Ágata Jiménez, is a strong and resourceful member of the band of the robbers. She is initially in charge of directing the mint operation and gets recognized for her strong leadership skills and unyielding will.

Nairobi is deeply committed to her team and, in many instances, acts as the moral guide, ensuring their safety even in the midst of chaos. Her backstory shows her difficulties as a single mother, thus rounding off her character. As the story progresses, she becomes a symbol of perseverance and empowerment, inspiring her fellow bank robbers to remain together.

Berlin - The Charismatic Antagonist

Berlin, whose real name is Andrés de Fonollosa, is the Professor's brother, one of the key players in the heist. Berlin is magnetic and multifaceted, one of which is his talent for being theatrical and often a leader in the group. He is indeed very barbaric and at the same time, a man of integrity, which makes him both endearing and divisive as a character.

This information on his terminal illness makes his character a more tragic figure, consequently altering his interactions with other characters. Reliving the memories of Berlin's past, which includes his encounters with love and betrayal, is of great significance in the storyline.

Helsinki - The Loyal Enforcer

Helsinki, whose real name is Mirko Dragic, is a former soldier and the most physically powerful guy in the heist crew. Indeed, it is his characteristic devotion to the group and the protective nature of his character that Helsinki, the muscle, provides both physical security and support in difficult situations.

His, and Oslo, the other crew member, relationship is one of the heart-touching elements in the series and it is a conduit through which the themes of friendship and sacrifice are shown. Although he is mainly rough and tough, Helsinki sometimes shows his gentle side, especially when it comes to his friends, thus he's a well-chosen character in the team.
